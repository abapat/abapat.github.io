Assignments

Assignment 1: Doodles

Use the principles reviewed in class to hypothesize a problem and a solution.
Doodle, sketch, storyboard, or write up the solution and upload to your page. If this is a GUI product - sketch mock-ups of the UI. Annotate your use of affordances, signifiers, cognitive models. Write who would you like to test your hypothesized technology with, and how. Think how you would evaluate success or failure.
