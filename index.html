About Me

Hi I'm Amit Bapat!
I am a software engineer currently working at VMware in Cloud Native Apps. I work on Microservices, Kubernetes, and Identity. I previously worked on Lightwave, an open-source project composed of enterprise-grade Identity Management and Directory Services aimed to provide security and governance on container workloads. Currently, I work on developing microservices in Go to solve some of the challenges in managing identity in Kubernetes.

I graduated from Stony Brook University in May 2017 with my Masters in Computer Science, with research focused on systems security. I also received my Bachelor's degree from Stony Brook University in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. Outside of class, I participated in various Hackathons and enjoy working on side-projects involving hardware and microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi). Within Computer Science, I'm interested in Distributed Systems, Security & Identity, Graph theory, and exploring new topics!
